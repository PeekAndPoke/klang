**Decision (2026-04-03, updated):** Pursue R42 Games Accelerator application for the 2027 cohort. Deadline: November 30,
2026.

**Framing strategy (revised):** Unified multi-market engine narrative. "One engine, three markets." Klang positioned as:

1. B2B game audio middleware (reactive, embeddable, two-way binding)
2. Gamified music production / synthesis education (intrinsic gamification -- the sound IS the reward)
3. Gamified coding education through music (programming concepts become audible)

**Previous framing (superseded):** Pure game audio middleware angle. Still strong but alone it was MODERATE fit. Adding
the education dimension upgrades to STRONG fit because it maps to R42's "serious games" and "gamification" categories,
validated by the Feynarock precedent (AI pen-and-paper RPG for kids, won Leipzig Start-up Prize).

**Key strategic insight:** Klang's education model is INTRINSIC gamification (the learning activity IS the play
activity), not extrinsic (badges/points on lessons). This is a more defensible and honest position.

**Two demos to build before November:**

1. Game integration demo (browser game + Klang audio reacting to game state)
2. "Zero to sound in 60 seconds" onboarding experience (education product demo)

**New action item:** Explore informal educational pilot with Leipzig music school or coding workshop before application.

**Why:** The multi-market story scores higher on R42's jury criteria: stronger market opportunities (three markets, not
one), higher innovation content (unique intersection), better program alignment (games + serious games + B2B).

**How to apply:** Full analysis saved to `.claude/vision/r42-accelerator-analysis.md`. First step unchanged: contact R42
with updated multi-market framing.
